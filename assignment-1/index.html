Threat People with Kindness

Harry Style

“It’s about being a lot nicer to each other rather than, ‘Don’t do this, or don’t do that, not this yes that.’ It’s just saying, ‘Treat people with kindness,’ you know.” -Harry Style

Choosing “Treat People With Kindness” was actually an easy decision for this project because it represents more than just a melody; it is a lifestyle. I picked this track to highlight how Harry Styles transformed a simple tour slogan into a global movement of radical empathy. In a world that often feels cynical, this song serves as a vibrant reminder that being nice is a choice we make every single day. I love how the vintage gospel choir elements create an atmosphere of collective joy, making the listener feel part of something bigger. By featuring this song, I wanted to capture that specific energy, the courage to be kind even when it is difficult. It resonates with me because it balances self-love with outward compassion, proving that we can only truly uplift others once we feel good in our own skin.

Harry Styles is an English singer, songwriter, and actor who first rose to fame as a member of the hugely successful boy band One Direction. After the band went on hiatus, he began a solo career that has made him one of the most popular artists in contemporary pop music. Styles is known for blending classic rock, pop, and soul influences into his music, and for his charismatic stage presence. His work often emphasizes themes of love, self-expression, and personal growth. Styles has received critical acclaim and a large international fan base. One of his notable messages, “Treat People With Kindness,” became a mantra during his live shows and later inspired the song of the same name.
